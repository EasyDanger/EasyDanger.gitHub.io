About ED

I've always been interested in making things. Whether writing stories, building things out of Lego, or cutting and stacking tree branches in the backyard, I've always been building something, even if I didn't yet know what it was. My appreciation for coding grew out of my need to create. I'm always excited to take on a new projects and to expand my knowledge base. I look forward to collaborating on new and challenging problems.

I was born and raised in Detroit. I graduated from Wayne State University. I will always be concerned and passionate about my city.

I believe that people should have a balanced, healthy lifestyle. To that end, I began running to further develop healthy habits and encourage my loved ones and community to do the same.

In addition to my programing and running community work, I occasionally host events.
